🚗 Smart Accident Detection System

Saving lives through instant automated response

⚠️ Problem Statement

Road accidents often go undetected in critical moments. Delays in emergency response significantly decrease survival chances and can lead to secondary collisions.

💡 Our Solution

An integrated IoT system that automatically detects impacts, captures health vitals (SpO₂), and broadcasts live location to hospitals and nearby vehicles instantly.

🔧 How the System Works

01

Detection

Gyroscope monitors orientation & impact. ESP32 processes anomalies instantly.

02

Vitals & GPS

GPS grabs coordinates while sensors measure victim's Oxygen (SpO₂).

03

Alerts

GSM sends data to family/hospitals. Nearby vehicles are alerted via local signal.

🛠️ Components

  • ESP32: The Brain (Microcontroller)
  • GSM Module: Emergency SMS/Alerts
  • GPS Module: Real-time Location
  • Gyroscope: Collision Detection
  • L298N: Vehicle Control/Safe Stop
